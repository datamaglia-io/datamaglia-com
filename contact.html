Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fractional CTO?

A Fractional CTO provides strategic technology leadership on a part-time basis, typically 1-2 days per week. This gives you access to C-level expertise without the full-time commitment and cost.

How quickly can we start?

Most engagements begin within 1-2 weeks of agreement. For urgent situations, we can often start within days.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We work with companies ranging from early-stage startups to large enterprises. Our approach is tailored to your company's size and growth stage.

What industries do you serve?

We have deep experience in FinTech, HealthTech, E-commerce, Industrial IoT, and SaaS platforms. Our expertise applies across many other industries as well.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, we offer both remote and on-site engagements. Many of our clients prefer a hybrid approach with regular on-site visits complemented by remote collaboration.

What's included in the initial consultation?

Our 30-minute consultation includes discussion of your current challenges, technology goals, and how our services might fit your needs. There's no obligation or cost.
